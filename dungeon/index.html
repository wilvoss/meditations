Dungeon settings

This is a dungeon crawler game without monsters that works best with a keyboard and mouse. It's based on Avatar, found on the Plato OS.

You can use the ↑ key/button to move forward, and ← → keys/buttons to reorient.

OPEN CONTROLS PANEL

A couple of things to note:

  1. Navigate with arrows. Move through doors by holding down the shift key.
  2. Adding a "#" and the level number to the end of the url will take you directly to that level. This can be handy if you want to bookmark a specific level.
  3. Pressing 1 - 0 (where 0 = 10) switches to that map, ctl1 - ctl6 correspond to levels 11-16. The letters w, p, h, g, l, and i work as expected.
  4. The theme, grid, and level states that you leave the map in are preserved in a cookie. The only thing that overrides this is #1, and that only effects the level, all other states are preserved.
